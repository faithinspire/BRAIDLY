About Box Braids

Box braids are individual plaits that are divided by small squared off parts or boxes. This iconic protective style has been a staple in African and African-American culture for decades.

Benefits:
  • Low maintenance protective style
  • Lasts 6-8 weeks with proper care
  • Versatile styling options
  • Promotes hair growth
  • Available in various lengths and colors

4-8 hours depending on length and size
