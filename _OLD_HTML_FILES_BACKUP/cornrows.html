About Cornrows

Cornrows are a traditional African hairstyle where the hair is braided very close to the scalp in continuous, raised rows. This timeless style is both practical and beautiful.

Benefits:
  • Low maintenance
  • Lasts 2-4 weeks
  • Versatile patterns and designs
  • Great for all hair types
  • Perfect for active lifestyles
Popular Styles:
  • Straight back cornrows
  • Feed-in cornrows
  • Curved/zigzag patterns
  • Cornrow updo
  • Side cornrows
Typical Duration:

2-5 hours depending on complexity

Price Range:

$80 - $200
