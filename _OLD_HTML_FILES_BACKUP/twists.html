About Twists

Twists are a protective hairstyle where two strands of hair are wrapped around each other. They're versatile, stylish, and perfect for natural hair care.

Benefits:
  • Promotes hair growth
  • Low manipulation
  • Lasts 3-6 weeks
  • Versatile styling options
  • Works on all hair lengths
Popular Styles:
  • Senegalese twists
  • Marley twists
  • Havana twists
  • Passion twists
  • Two-strand twists
Typical Duration:

3-6 hours depending on length and size

Price Range:

$100 - $250
